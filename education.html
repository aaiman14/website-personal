My Education Journey

Gombak,Selangor

My educational journey began at SK Taman Seri Gombak a primary school located in Selangor, where I laid the foundation for my academic growth. During these formative years, I developed a strong passion for learning and began cultivating curiosity and critical thinking skills. I participated in various activities, such as sports and class projects, which helped me develop social skills and teamwork. The positive environment in primary school nurtured my love for knowledge and set the stage for my future academic endeavors.

Merlimau,Melaka

After completing my primary education, I continued my studies at SMI Al-Amin Bestari D'Merlimau in Melaka for my secondary education and where I took my SPM. This was a transformative period where I not only focused on academic excellence but also participated in a variety of extracurricular activities. I further honed my skills in leadership, communication, and problem-solving while being part of various clubs and events. Secondary school challenged me intellectually and personally, helping me explore my interests and passions and laying the groundwork for my choice of career in Information Management.

Rembau,Negeri Sembilan

Currently, I am pursuing higher education at UiTM Campus Rembau in Negeri Sembilan, specializing in Information Management. At university, my academic experience has been a journey of discovery and self-improvement. The comprehensive courses, coupled with hands-on learning, have equipped me with the technical skills and theoretical knowledge needed for a career in the dynamic field of Information Management. University has not only deepened my understanding of technology and data management but also provided me with opportunities for personal growth through leadership roles and student organizations. I look forward to completing my studies and stepping into the professional world with confidence.
